ABOUT

I am Olesya Nykytenko and I am a passionate photographer who sees beauty in every fraction of a human being. I will catch the best moments of an important part of your life and make them memorable and long-lasting. I will share the happy moments with you and make the best photos ever. As the photo is not just a fancy picture, it's a specter of emotions caught in a right moment. I specialize in wedding, love stories, events and children's photo sessions, but if you have any ideas for the photo session, I would happily discuss with you the possibilities of collaboration.
